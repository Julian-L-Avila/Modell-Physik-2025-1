All data has been collected from a reference point P,
located at the bottom-left corner of the LEGO base.
P1 corresponds to angle Î± (alpha), which is located at the bottom-right corner
of the LEGO base.
P2 corresponds to the angle located at the bottom-left corner of the LEGO base.

The naming format used for the files is as follows:
D0(VideoName)_two(same video recorded twice)_data_P1(measured angle)

Please note:
The data from D10_two_data_P1 and D10_two_data_P2 may be affected due to camera
movement during the recording.
